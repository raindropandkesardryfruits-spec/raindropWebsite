About Raindrop Kesar And Dry Fruits

Raindrop Kesar And Dry Fruits is your trusted destination for high-quality, handpicked dry fruits straight from the heart of Kashmir. Founded by Sami Ullah Akbar, our brand is built on purity, tradition, and a commitment to healthy living.

We ensure every almond, raisin, and cashew is selected with precision and packed with care to retain its natural taste and nutrition. Our products are perfect for daily snacking, gifting, and festive celebrations.
